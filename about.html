Iffat’s Rannaghor was inspired by the warmth of home cooking and the rich traditions of Bengali cuisine. Growing up, the kitchen was always the heart of the home, where family gathered around the table to share stories, laughter, and comforting meals. The word rannaghor means “kitchen” in Bengali, and this restaurant was created to bring that same feeling of home to everyone who visits.

Our menu celebrates authentic Bengali flavors, from fragrant Kacchi Biryani to classic dishes like Ilish Bhapa and Chingri Malai Curry. Each dish is prepared with traditional spices, fresh ingredients, and recipes passed down through generations.

At Iffat’s Rannaghor, our mission is simple: to share the comfort and culture of Bengali food with our community. Whether you are trying these dishes for the first time or they remind you of home, we hope every meal here feels warm, welcoming, and memorable.
